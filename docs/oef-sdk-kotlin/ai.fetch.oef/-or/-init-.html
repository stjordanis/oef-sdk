Or(constraints: List<ConstraintExpr>)

A constraint type that allows you to specify a disjunction of constraints. That is, the Or constraint is satisfied whenever at least one of the constraints that constitute the or is satisfied.

Example: All the books that have been published either before the year 1960 or after the year 1970

val c = Or(listOf(Constraint("year", Relation.LT(1960)), Constraint("year", Relation.GT(1970))))
